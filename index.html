ABOUT US

DTU BattleGround provides a platform to the various societies, faculty and students of Delhi Technological University to host private coding competitions online. With a feature-rich IDE supporting widely used programming languages like C++, Python, and Java, DTU BattleGround ensures a perfect environment to the budding coders.

DTU BattleGround can be used to host competitive contests, practice events, or can act as a platform for coding assignments submission. It also acts as a gateway for students looking for opportunities to sharpen their programming skills. We plan to extend our operations outside DTU in near future.
